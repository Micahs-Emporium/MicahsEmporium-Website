The Living Room

17th July 2025 at 5:21pm

Even hardworking folk yearn for a soft couch and a warm hearth every now and then.
A space for the family to gather, away from the ever changing and busy main hall below. Irith and Ahaish hadn't thought of granting themselves a space like this before the triplet of trouble makers showed up on their doorstep. A quiet and cozy room to sleep was all they needed.
However, with the little rascals running around, all three trying their best to get to know a new town and settle into their new routine, it proved far more comfortable for everyone involved to take up a little more space in the attic for family alone.
