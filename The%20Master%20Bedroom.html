The Master Bedroom

17th July 2025 at 5:39pm

Sometimes, what happens behind closed doors is exactly what you expect.
The first major refurbished space of the upstairs was Irith's and Ahaish's bedroom. A space to themselves, away from the bustle downstairs, but close enough for Irith to jump down and help wherever needed.
The 'Tavern' is the villages central gathering space, and all are welcome to come and use its commodities. And though it is not a business that Irith herself owns, rest is not in her blood and she will take even the smallest shifting of pans or creaking of doors as an excuse to leave the restful bedroom and head downstairs.
