The Kids Bedroom

17th July 2025 at 5:20pm

If it werent for the two walls that turned one room to three, the tavern would be a much less serene space.

Ir, Ish and Hai, the three kids of Irith and Ahaish each have their own room upstairs in the tavern. Though the contents of each room is kept as similar as possible (lest there be sibling rivalry, and outcries of willful neglect and unfairness) simply the sound and smell originating from each bedroom door would tell you which little human resides within.
