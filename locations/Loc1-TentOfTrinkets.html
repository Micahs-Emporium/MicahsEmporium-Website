The tent of Trinkets

A merchant who has set up shop in the desert, right at the shortest water crossover between it and the nearby grasslands.
His tent, thick enough to keep the rough desert sand out, but tattered enough to show its age, is filled to the brim with wares no humenoid should ever lay eyes on.

The vial comes with some food flakes for the pixie that can be fed through the holes of the shaker.
