The Split Village

A long fall and rush down from the Nich'kura mountains lies Tarr, a small mountain village split in two by a deep riverbed.
One which lies dry for most of the year, and serves the town as a main road. For the brief few months where the melting glacier ice fills the river with rushing water, it splits the village in two.
Thynemos and Arjal, the two ground folk who serve and lead the community, each take half of Tarr under their wing when the raging stream divides the village.
