The Mountain Ridge of the Nomads

A wonderfull sight throughout this world sanning mountain ridge are the nomads crossing its many relentless peaks.
Dotted around, every few hundred miles, are their sacred waypoints; Small one-building settlements ran by the wisest of the elders.
These waypoints provide shelter for those unable to continue the nomadic lifestyle, due to illness, pregnancy or other, but they also play a huge roll in the resupplying of the passing caravans.
