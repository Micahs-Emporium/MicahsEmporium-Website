The Master Bedroom

Even the owner of a dayround gathering place needs plenty of rest and privacy.
The shell of this house was made far before Irith and Agaish moved in, however the interior is most their own. Agaish' Woodworking skills granted them a graceful, and most importantly sturdy, bed.
This along with matching sidetables, a chest for heavy personal items and some other bits and bobs for clothing, belongings and more.
