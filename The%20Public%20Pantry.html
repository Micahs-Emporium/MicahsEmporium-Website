The Public Pantry

17th July 2025 at 5:19pm

Labour kept is labour shared. Effort makes not waste.
The newest edition to this haven is the shed erected on its side. Where previously unused inverntory was stashed and forgotten about in the upper floor of the tavern, it now lives in the pantry. A place where all in Tharr can access them, giving them a second life.

It was a build brought to life when Irith and Ahaish moved into the large building and wished for space upstairs for themselves. Now only the seasonal and bulk items like firewood and Cider barrels live upstairs. The rest gets left, taken or exchanged in the public pantry. No product of labour going to waste.
