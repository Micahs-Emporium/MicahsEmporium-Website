The Hearth of the Home
A simple wood-fueled stove and a some good sturdy shelves and barrels to support the everyday ingredients. At the back of the kitchen: a very well isolated storage chamber for the bulk goods that don't need to be stored out in the food pits. Salt-dried meats, oven-dried fruits and frost-dried vegetables. Walls lined with deer legs, strings of garlic and rows and rows of braided corn cobs, their cernels in hues of reds, whites and purples.
