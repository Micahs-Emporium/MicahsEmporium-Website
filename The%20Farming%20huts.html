The Farming huts

28th July 2025 at 8:55am

The greening of the land is a gift. Take it. with both hands and a deep bow.
The fertile land beneath the splitting river is available to locals for a great part of the year. However, not all this time falls within the growing season.
Thus swift and well planned action is needed to make the most of this valuable time.
Part of this is achieved by the people of Tharr settled on each side of the river, keeping up the care for the fencing.
Another by those proficient in woodworking keeping the sheds ready and stocked with sharp tools and necessary materials. Bulk items may be kept at the Shepherds Well, but the farming huts dotted around the river need to be stocked with the essentials, to always be ready to go.
